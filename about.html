About Us:

Bake and Bite cafe is a traditional family owned cafe owned and run by Ganesh Lama, Nier Lama, Rekha Shahi and Usha Khadgi. The passion and love for food has always been number one in our family as grew up in a family where both our mother and father loved cooking good food for us. Food is a symbol of love and joy in our family and our parents always loved sharing joy in the form of food with other people, so we continued in their footsteps and that is how we came up with this cafe. We took this love for food a little further and thought about sharing it with other people.

Food is an important part of our well being and it is very important to eat good, healthy and nutritious food so we combined all these together along with excellent taste and came up with some excellent reciepes. This cafe is much more than just business for us. It is about making a difference which is why we are dedicated to providing our customers with good food that are prepared using high quality ingredients and comes with affordale prices. We also make foods as per the customers request keeping their taste, preferences, choices and health in mind. The main idea behind this cafe is to spread a little joy in your life through food. So, weather it is a cup of coffee or great food, come visit us and enjoy the unique atomesphere here at the Bake & Bite Cafe.
