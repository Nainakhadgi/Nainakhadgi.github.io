Bake & Bite Cafe

WELCOME!

Bake and Bite cafe is a traditional family owned business brought to the food loving people by the ones who love eating and making good food themselves. This cafe was established with the motive of delivering people with our unique food creations, and the comfort food that we love eating. We are dedicated to providing our customers with good food that are prepared using high quality ingredients and comes with affordale prices. We serve coffees, lattes, espressos along with our specialty bakery items and a variety of Newari, Chinese, Italian and American, Indian cuisines. We also make customized cakes for different occasions.

Some of Our Specialities:

Mud Cake with Ice-cream

Mud cake with ice-cream is one of our signature dish here. It is a perfect blend of cold ice-cream on top of hot mud cake.

Samay Baji

Samay baji is an authentic traditional Newari dish. It is a platter which consists of various dishes such as baji (beaten rice), bhatmas (soybeans), aanda (boiled egg), choila (spiced meat), saag (spinach), bodi (boiled beans), sandheko aloo (spiced potato) and ginger/garlic slices in a single plate.

Caramel Latte

This is one of our go to drinks here. It comes in different sizes (small, medium or large) and can be hot or cold.

Hot Garlic Mo:Mo

Hot garlic Mo:Mo is one of our customer favorites. We tried to bring a Chinese twist to the famous Nepali dish and people loved it! Give it a try, you will also love it for sure!
